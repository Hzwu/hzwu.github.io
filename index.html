Hanzhou Wu

I specialize in INFORMATION HIDING AND ITS APPLICATIONS. I once studied or worked at Southwest Jiaotong University (with Professor Hongxia Wang),

New Jersey Institute of Technology (with Professor Yun-Qing Shi), Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (with Professor Jing Dong),

and Shanghai University (with Professor Xinpeng Zhang). Now, I work with a team of my graduate students at Shanghai University.

My research interest always covers mainstream and cutting-edge topics. I am 35 years old, living with my wife and daughter.
